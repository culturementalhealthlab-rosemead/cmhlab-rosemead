About Our Lab

The Culture and Mental Health Lab focuses on the interactions between cultural factors and mental health variables, and is co-directed by Eu Gene Chin and Carissa Dwiwardani. Studies in the lab include both basic and applied research to improve accessibility to mental health services among communities of color.

Asian American Specialty Training Initiative

One key focus of the CMH Lab is our Asian American Specialty Training Initiative (AASTI). This initiative is designed to address the specific mental health needs of Asian American communities through specialized training, dedicated research, and community-oriented outreach.

The AASTI aims to cultivate a new generation of mental health professionals who are proficient in providing culturally responsive care. Our work within this initiative includes providing advanced practicum consultation (RSPY668), co-hosting the 2026 Center for Asian American Christianity (CAAC) Biola Mental Health Conference, and conducting ongoing program evaluation and research activities.
